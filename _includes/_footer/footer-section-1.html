About Us

Siam Editing is a UK and Ireland based editing group, providing an academic editing service to academics, doctoral / masters students and journal / book publishers. Our editors are educated to doctoral level and are native English speakers. We accept projects from both domestic and international clients.

Our Philosophy

We aim to help non-native students present their dissertations in an impressive, professional manner whereby they can be confident that their written English ability will not be a factor in how their project is graded. Likewise, we aim to ensure that the research efforts of academics are presented in the best light possible to journal referees. It should be pointed out that we operate a policy of strict confidentiality.
