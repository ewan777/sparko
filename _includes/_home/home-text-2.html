Pricing

Siam Editing offers a professional service and our pricing is very competitive. Each project is considered individually, and an estimate given accordingly, though prices generally increase with the size and complexity of the project. For example, in the case of undergraduate and masters dissertations, there is a service charge of 60 GBP, and an editing fee of 5 GBP per 1000 words. For doctoral dissertations and academic manuscripts, there is a service charge of 60 GBP, and an editing fee of 8 GBP per 1000 words.
